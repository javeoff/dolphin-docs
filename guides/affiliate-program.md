# Affiliate Program

Earn commissions by referring new users to Dolphin Anty.

## Commission structure

You earn **15% of spending** from users you refer. Commissions are calculated based on the amount your referrals spend in their Dolphin Anty personal area.

## Eligibility

- You must be an existing Dolphin Anty user
- One referral link per account
- You cannot use third-party paid promotion services on Dolphin Anty's behalf

## Getting your referral link

1. Log in to your Dolphin Anty personal area at [dolphin-anty.com](https://dolphin-anty.com)
2. Go to the referral section
3. Copy your unique referral link

Share this link with potential users. When they register and pay, you receive commissions.

## Payment schedule

- Commissions are calculated and credited **weekly**
- After a minimum of 1 calendar week, you can withdraw bonuses by contacting support
- Bonuses cannot be transferred between Dolphin Anty accounts

## Allowed promotion methods

- Website or blog articles with your referral link
- Social media posts
- Banner advertisements
- Video descriptions (YouTube, etc.)
- Media articles and reviews
- Review platform listings
- Contextual advertising (non-branded keywords only)
- Forum signatures

## Prohibited practices

- Spamming
- Using Dolphin Anty's brand name or product name as paid ad keywords
- Claiming employment or official partnership status beyond "official partner"
- Using Dolphin Anty domain names in ad URLs
- Fraudulent referral requests
