# Pricing Plans

Dolphin Anty offers five plans, from a free tier to a fully customizable enterprise option.

## Plans overview

| Plan | Price | Profiles | Users |
|------|-------|----------|-------|
| Free | $0/month | 5 | 1 |
| Starter | $10/month | 60 | 1 |
| Base | $89/month | 100 | 1 (+$10 each) |
| Team | $159/month | 300 | 1 (+$20 each) |
| Enterprise | $299/month | 1,000–50,000+ | 1 (+$25 each) |
| Custom | Contact sales | Unlimited | Unlimited |

## Plan details

### Free
- 5 browser profiles
- 1 user
- Local profile storage only
- No API access

### Starter — $10/month
- 60 browser profiles
- 1 user
- 1,500 API requests per minute
- Local and cloud sync
- Profile synchronizer

### Base — $89/month
Everything in Starter, plus:
- 100 browser profiles
- Additional users at $10 each
- Switch between 2 accounts
- Scenario builder (Script Builder)
- Profile trash bin (48-hour recovery)
- Bulk fingerprint refresh

### Team — $159/month
Everything in Base, plus:
- 300 browser profiles
- Additional users at $20 each
- Switch between 3 accounts
- Full team collaboration features

### Enterprise — $299/month
Everything in Team, plus:
- 1,000 to 50,000+ browser profiles
- Additional users at $25 each
- Switch between 10 accounts
- Dedicated account managers
- Maximum automation access

### Custom
For large operations needing unlimited profiles. Pricing is personalized based on your requirements — contact the sales team.

## Common questions

**How many users are included by default?**
Each plan includes 1 user (the account owner). Additional users must be purchased separately.

**Can I access the same account from multiple devices?**
Yes. Multiple people can log in to the same account simultaneously without penalty.

**What is the minimum subscription period?**
One month. There are no annual commitments required, though longer terms may be available.

**Can I recover deleted profiles?**
Free and Starter plans permanently delete profiles immediately. Base, Team, and Enterprise plans offer a trash bin with a 48-hour recovery window.
